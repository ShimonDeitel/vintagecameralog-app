Privacy Policy

Vintage Camera Log is an offline-first app. All camera data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's own storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data, and the app makes no network requests of its own.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment information.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. There is no server-side account or backup.
